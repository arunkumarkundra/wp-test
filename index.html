Waypoint · Vacation Intelligence · free time-aware engine

Don't search for a trip. Decide one.

Tell Waypoint where you're starting from — any town on Earth. It discovers candidates, weighs the time you actually have against the time each place deserves, checks real weather for your month, your holiday calendar, and prices the whole trip — then ranks everything around your budget, pace and comfort. Completely free. No login. Nothing you enter is stored on any server — your trip stays on your device.

Time is the real constraintWe weigh the days you have against the days a place deserves, and the journey each way. If travel would eat your trip, we say so — and how many extra days unlock it.
